About Me

I am a research engineer at the Search Recommendation Center of Social Platform and Application Line, Tencent, since 2021. My work mainly focuses on optimizing deep models for short video recommendations in QQ, using large-scale user behavior data.

Before joining Tencent, I received my Master's degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, in 2021 where I was involved in the research of computer vision applied on remote sensing images.

Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer and Information Engineering, Henan University, in 2018 where I got the qualification for the recommended postgraduate with a GPA of 3.69/4 (rank 2/87).
