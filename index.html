About

I am a research engineer at Search Recommendation Center of Social Platforms and Application Line, Tencent since 2021. And my work mainly focus on optimizing recommendation models based on massive user behavior data for short video recommendation in QQ.

Before joining Tencent, I received my Master's degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology, ShenZhen in 2021 where I was engaged in the research of computer version applied on remote sensing images.

Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from School of Computer and Information Engineering, Henan University in 2018 where I got the qualification for the recommended postgraduate with GPA 3.78 (Rank 2/87).
